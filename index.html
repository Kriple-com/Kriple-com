Why choose Kriple?

We built Kriple because accepting crypto should be as simple as accepting a card. Merchants deserve full payment amounts — no platform fees skimmed per transaction. Our model is a flat monthly subscription so you always know your costs. Fast, transparent, and designed to scale with your business.

Security isn't optional. It's foundational.

Every transaction flows through bank-grade AES encryption. KYC powered by SumSub for merchant compliance. Two-factor authentication with TOTP for every admin account. Blockchain verification and signature-protected webhooks on every payment event.

Crypto payments are inherently private by design — your wallet address is linked only to the transaction, never to a personal identity. Kriple respects and preserves this privacy-first nature of blockchain: we never expose or sell on-chain activity data.

Built for merchants worldwide

From solo entrepreneurs to growing e-commerce businesses, merchants around the world use Kriple to accept crypto with confidence. Our infrastructure monitors blockchain events in real time and instant on-chain settlement.
