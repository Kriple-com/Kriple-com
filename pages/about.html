About Us

Here starts the future of crypto

Our Mission

Kriple started with a clear goal in mind: to make cryptocurrency payments easy to use, fully transparent, and available to businesses of any size. We’re convinced that accepting crypto should feel just as straightforward as taking card payments — while allowing merchants to keep every satoshi they earn.

Our Story

Everything started in 2026 when Kriple was created to solve a real problem in the market. Many businesses interested in crypto payments struggled with disconnected tools, expensive transaction fees, and complicated integrations. Our team decided to simplify everything by building one seamless platform that covers it all — from hosted checkout to KYC compliance and recurring billing.

Today, Kriple aims to support merchants in various countries, enabling them to accept cryptocurrency payments across networks like Solana, Ethereum, Polygon, Avalanche, and BNB Chain. With the release of API Version 2.0, we introduced a flat monthly pricing model, eliminating per-transaction fees altogether.

Our Values

  • Transparency — Flat monthly pricing, no hidden fees per transaction
  • Security — AES encryption, KYC compliance, TOTP admin auth
  • Simplicity — Complex blockchain infrastructure, simple merchant experience
  • Innovation — Multi-chain support, smart contract subscriptions, ML analytics
